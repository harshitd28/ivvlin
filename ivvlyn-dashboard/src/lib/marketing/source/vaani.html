STEP 3 — FOLLOWING UP SO YOU DON'T HAVE TO

Most leads need 5-7 touches before they're ready to buy. Vaani sends all of them.

Following up means reaching out to a lead multiple times over several days or weeks until they either buy or say they're not interested. It's the most important part of sales — and the most forgotten.

Your sales team follows up once, maybe twice. Then they move on. The lead goes cold. Vaani follows up 6 times over 14 days across WhatsApp, SMS, and Email. Each message is different. Each one is timed based on when the lead is most likely to respond. Never spammy. Always relevant.

After Day 14, Vaani marks the lead as cold and notifies your team. Clean. Organised. Nothing falls through the cracks.

STEP 4 — BOOKING THE PROPERTY VISIT

Vaani fills your weekend calendar without you lifting a finger.

When a lead says they want to see the property, Vaani immediately sends them available time slots, confirms the visit, and saves it to your calendar. The day before the visit — she sends the lead a reminder so they don't forget. The morning of — she reminds your sales team who they're meeting and what that lead cares about. No missed visits. No awkward "I forgot" calls.
